What's in a Name?

The scientific name of a frog is Anura. This special name helps scientists talk about frogs without getting confused.

Frog Classification

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Amphibia
  • Order: Anura

Frog Families

There are over 5,000 species of frogs, including true frogs, tree frogs, and toads.

Fun Fact

The word Anura means 'without a tail' in Greek. Adult frogs don't have tails, but tadpoles do!
