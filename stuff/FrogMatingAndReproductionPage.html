Frog Life Cycle

Did you know that frogs have a very interesting way of growing up? Let's find out how!

  • Eggs: It all starts when a mommy frog lays lots of tiny eggs in the water.
  • Tadpoles: These eggs hatch into tadpoles, baby frogs that look like little fish.
  • Growing Up: Tadpoles grow legs and lose their tails as they turn into adult frogs.
  • Adult Frogs: They can now hop on land and swim in water, without tails.

Frog Families

Mommy and daddy frogs don't stay with their babies. The tadpoles learn to take care of themselves.

Fun Facts

Frogs lay hundreds of eggs at once, and the time it takes for a tadpole to become a frog varies.
