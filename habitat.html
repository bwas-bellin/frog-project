Frog Habitats Explained

Frogs can be found in many places around the world. They love wet and moist environments.

  • Ponds and Lakes: Many frogs live in or near still and calm waters.
  • Streams and Rivers: Some prefer moving waters like streams and rivers.
  • Wetlands: Wetlands are marshy areas loved by frogs.
  • Forests and Jungles: Frogs also live in damp areas in forests and jungles.

Froggy Homes

Frogs need water for living and laying eggs, and some even live up in trees!
