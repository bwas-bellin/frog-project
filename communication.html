The Language of Frogs

  • Croaking: Frogs croak to communicate different messages.
  • Body Language: Frogs use body movements to express themselves.
  • Color Changes: Some frogs change colors to show their feelings.

Why Do Frogs Croak?

Frogs croak to find mates, talk to each other, and warn of danger.

Listening to Frogs

Frogs have good hearing to listen to other frogs and detect danger.

Fun Activity
